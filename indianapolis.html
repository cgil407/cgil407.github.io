Indianapolis, Indiana

Nestled between the banks of the two forked White River, Indianapolis is where history and modern marvels meet. Home to the Indianapolis Motor Speedway, Lucas Oil Stadium and the nations largest children's museum, the city has something for everyone to enjoy.

Facts about Indianapolis:

  • City Population: 887,642
  • Incorporated: 1821
  • Region: East North Central region of the Midwest
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $66,629
